## After Activation: Start Lip-sync Control

Once activation is completed:

### Step 1: Initiating Lip-Sync

<img
  src="/images/activatinglipsync_02.png"
  alt="activatinglipsync_02.png"
  title="activatinglipsync_02.png"
  style={{ width:"58%" }}
/>

- Click **Start Lip-Sync Control** to enable synchronization. It will take **7-8 seconds**.
- **Allow microphone permission** when prompted.

### Step 2: Enable Voice Isolation

<img
  src="/images/activatinglipsync_03.png"
  alt="activatinglipsync_03.png"
  title="activatinglipsync_03.png"
  style={{ width:"57%" }}
/>

Enable the Voice Isolation setting to ensure clear audio input. This is one-time setup, so you don't need to enable everytime you open the app. For details, see <u>What is Voice Isolation</u> below.

### Step 3: Using Lip-sync

<img
  src="/images/activatinglipsync_04.png"
  alt="activatinglipsync_04.png"
  title="activatinglipsync_04.png"
  style={{ width:"55%" }}
/>

Once started, speak into your microphone. Your AI Self will mirror your mouth movements with less than a 1-second delay.

## What is Pickle Delayed Voice for Lip-sync?

Utilizing this, you can ensure your audio and visual outputs align correctly when using your AI Self in external applications like Zoom:

> - When lip-sync is active, there is a ~0.7s delay before your AI Self mirrors your voice.
> - Opponents will experience mismatch between your audio and video.

To test the delayed audio output and see how it looks like to your opponent in Video calls, activate Preview Pickle Delayed Voice for Lip-sync on Speaker.

## What is Voice Isolation?

Voice Isolation prevents your microphone from capturing opponent's speaking audio. Effective when using built-in microphones (e.g., MacBook Pro Microphone) instead of earbuds. It's saved setting, so you don't need to enable everytime.

<img
  src="/images/activatinglipsync_08.png"
  alt="activatinglipsync_08.png"
  title="activatinglipsync_08.png"
  style={{ width:"46%" }}

To activate, 

1. Click on **orange <Icon icon="circle-microphone" iconType="solid" color="#ff9702" /> icon** on the top menu bar when lip-sync is on.
2. Click '**Mic Mode**' and select '**Voice Isolation**'.
